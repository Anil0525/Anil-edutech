🎨 Question Palette — Status Symbols
  • Not Visited — You have not visited the question yet.
  • Not Answered — You visited but did not answer the question.
  • Answered — You have answered and saved the question.
  • Marked for Review — Not answered, but marked to revisit.
  • Answered + Marked for Review — Answered and also marked to revisit. This answer will be evaluated.
⚠️ Note: If a question is answered and marked for review, the answer will be considered for evaluation. "Mark for Review" simply means you want to revisit it.
🧭 Navigating & Answering Questions
  1. Click on the question number in the Question Palette on the right to go to any question directly. (This does NOT save your current answer.)
  2. Click Save & Next to save your answer and move to the next question.
  3. Click Mark for Review & Next to save your answer, mark it for review, and move to the next question.
  4. Click Clear Response to deselect your chosen answer.
  5. You can switch between Part-A, Part-B, Part-C, Part-D at any time using the Part tabs in the toolbar. The shared timer continues running.
  6. After the last question of a section, clicking Save & Next will automatically take you to the first question of the next section.
  7. All sections, except English Comprehension, can be attempted in both English and Hindi. You may change language per question.
  8. ONLY questions for which answers are saved or answered & marked for review will be considered for evaluation.
  9. Click Submit Test only when you are sure you want to end the exam. Once submitted, you cannot go back.
